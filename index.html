FACT SHEET

Description

Asteroid Hunter is a sci-fi RPG that takes place in a distant place, in the not-too-distant future.

Players assume the role of Astronaut Trainees reporting to Cyberspace Command to log the required amount of hours in training sims in preparation for their rotation aboard the Orbital Colony Space Station L5 as active-duty astronauts of the AstroTech Corps, tasked with keeping the galaxies clean and safe.

You are training, not gaming.

Blending a visual novel and an arcade game into a slice-of-life RPG this experience tests your curiosity, hand-eye coordination, and whether or not you’ve got what it takes to qualify for the space program.

Good luck, Space Cowboy!

Features

 Simulation:

    Log in to your Terminal, take jobs, complete tasks, and get rewarded for your progress and accomplishments.

 Experience:

    Practice piloting a drone through a high-speed space highway while operating the asteroid-busting weapons system.

 Learn:

    Take a break from simulation training and read first-hand accounts from other pilot and operator crews, gather knowledge from mission reports, memorize tips and tricks, and stayup to date on the latest news from around the galaxy
